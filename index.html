The Lion King

Released: June 24, 1994

Starring

  • Mathew Broderick
  • Jonathan Taylor Thomas
  • James Earl Jones
  • Jeremy Irons

Movie Quote

"Look at the stars. Th great kings of the past look down on us form those stars... So whenever you feel alone, just remember that those kings will always be there to guide you. And so will I." [source]

Plot

Mufasa, the king of Pride Rock has a son, Simba, making his brother Scar second in line to the throne. Scar hatches a plot to kill Mufasa and Simba, but when Simba survives, he convinces him that Mufasa's death was his fault, forcing Simba to run away. Simba grows up in the wilderness with a life of no worries. However, when he is reunited with a childhood friend, he is forced to go back and confront his uncle. After a battle between the two, it is revealed that Scar was responsible for Mufasa's death. Scar then causes his own death by leaping at Simbas as his back is turned but, instead of killing Simba, he gets thrown off of a cliff like Mufasa was.
